## Detailed Section Breakdown

### 1. Mariana Announcement Bar
**Purpose**: Create urgency, announce launch
**Key Elements**:
- Short, uppercase text
- Clear date/time
- Clickable (links to collection)
- Emoji for personality
**Spacing**: 1.2rem padding, thin border

---

### 2. Header (Enhanced)
**Purpose**: Navigation + branding
**Key Elements**:
- Logo centered or left
- Clean menu spacing
- Search + account + cart icons
**Spacing**: 3.2rem padding top/bottom

---

### 3. Mariana Hero Gallery
**Purpose**: Stop scroll, create desire
**Key Elements**:
- ONE stunning image (your best Christmas piece)
- Minimal text overlay
- Clear CTA button
- Full-width, high impact
**Content**:
- H1: "Christmas Resin Collection" (72px)
- Subtext: One sentence about the collection
- Button: "View Collection" or "Explore Pieces"
**Image specs**: 2000px Ã— 1333px, high quality

---

### 4. Rich Text - Artist Note (Optional)
**Purpose**: Add context, storytelling
**Key Elements**:
- 2-3 sentences max
- Center-aligned
- Generous padding
**Example**:
"This Christmas, I've created a small series of resin artworks inspired by the magic of the season. Each piece is one-of-a-kind, combining deep forest greens, shimmering golds, and ethereal whites. Once they're gone, they're gone forever."

---

### 5. Mariana Featured Collection
**Purpose**: Show the artwork, enable purchase
**Key Elements**:
- Grid of 3-4 products
- Square images (consistent!)
- Product title, medium, price
- "View All" CTA below
**Settings**:
- Collection: Christmas Resin Collection
- Products to show: 3 or 4
- Columns: 3 (desktop)
- Show view all: Yes

---

### 6. Existing Sections
**Keep what's working**:
- Your current "About" section
- Testimonials (Kate Ceberano, Ali McGregor, Teresa Palmer)
- Instagram feed
- Email signup

**Remove or minimize**:
- Anything that feels cluttered
- Multiple competing CTAs
- Long text blocks
- Busy banners

## Spacing Philosophy

### Desktop:
- **Section padding**: 8rem (80px) top/bottom
- **Grid gaps**: 6rem (60px) between products
- **Content max-width**: 90rem (900px) for text
- **Page max-width**: Default theme setting

### Mobile:
- **Section padding**: 5rem (50px) top/bottom
- **Grid gaps**: 4rem (40px) between products
- **Side margins**: 2rem (20px)

### Why generous spacing?
- Feels premium, not cramped
- Lets artwork breathe
- Gallery aesthetic, not shop
- Mobile-friendly tap targets

## Visual Hierarchy

```
Primary Focus:    Hero image (75% of viewport)
Secondary Focus:  Product images (large, generous spacing)
Tertiary Focus:   Text (minimal, supporting)
UI Elements:      Subtle, don't compete with art
```

**The artwork is the hero. Everything else supports it.**

---

## Color Usage

```
Background:    White / Off-white (let art pop)
Text:          Charcoal gray (not pure black)
Borders:       8-10% opacity (subtle)
Hover:         65% opacity (elegant fade)
Buttons:       Black text on white, or white on transparent
```

**Keep it minimal. The resin colors are the visual interest.**

---

## Launch Day Adjustments

### Before 8PM:
- Announcement: "Releasing Tonight 8PM"
- Products: Hidden or "Coming Soon"
- Hero: Teaser image

### At 8PM:
- Announcement: "Available Now" or "Shop Collection"
- Products: Live and purchasable
- Hero: Update to "Shop Now" if desired

### Day After:
- Announcement: "Only X Pieces Remaining"
- Products: Hide sold items or show "Sold" badge
- Hero: Update to show bestseller if one emerges
